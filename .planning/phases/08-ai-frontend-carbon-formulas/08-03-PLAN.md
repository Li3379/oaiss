GB/T 32150-2015 Power Grid Formula:
```
E = T_loss × EF_grid

Where:
  T_loss = V_transmission × L_rate  (transmission loss in MWh)
  EF_grid = grid emission factor (tCO2/MWh)

Expanded with all 9 parameters:

Core calculation:
  V_transmission = total transmission volume (MWh)
  L_rate = line loss rate (0-1)
  EF_grid = grid emission factor (tCO2/MWh)

T_loss = V_transmission × L_rate
E = T_loss × EF_grid

Additional detail parameters:
  V_generation = total generation volume (MWh)
  V_import = imported electricity (MWh)
  V_export = exported electricity (MWh)
  EF_import = emission factor for imported electricity (tCO2/MWh)
  reporting_year
  enterprise_name

9 parameters:
  1. transmissionVolume (V_transmission, MWh)
  2. lineLossRate (L_rate, 0-1)
  3. gridEmissionFactor (EF_grid, tCO2/MWh)
  4. generationVolume (V_generation, MWh) — reference/info
  5. importedElectricity (V_import, MWh) — reference/info
  6. exportedElectricity (V_export, MWh) — reference/info
  7. importEmissionFactor (EF_import, tCO2/MWh) — for imported electricity emissions
  8. reportingYear (Integer)
  9. enterpriseName (String)
```
