<objective>
Eliminate race conditions in financial transactions by ensuring distributed locks run before transactions and all financial entities have optimistic locking.

Purpose: Without CON-03, @Transactional can execute outside the distributed lock, allowing concurrent matching. Without CON-01, the synchronized keyword only works in single-instance deployments. Without CON-02, concurrent updates to financial entities silently overwrite each other (lost update anomaly).
Output: Lock-before-transaction guarantee, distributed lock on auction matching, optimistic lock on 3 financial entities.
</objective>
